Why Choose HemoScan?

Engineered for accuracy, designed for portability.

Detailed Diagnostics

Measures Hemoglobin (Hb) and automatically calculates Hematocrit (HCT). Includes Smart Anemia Classification (Normal, Mild, Moderate, Severe).

Results in < 10 Seconds

Fast, on-the-spot screening with audible feedback permits rapid clinical decision-making. Wakes instantly on button press.

Intuitive Interface

2.4-inch Color TFT LCD with a vibrant, easy-to-read interface. Simple 4-button navigation (Up, Down, Back, OK).

Powerful Battery

rechargeable 2600 mAh Li-ion battery. Delivers ~24 hours runtime. Intelligent auto-sleep to maximize battery life.

Smart Sampling

Uses a dedicated micro-cuvette tray with automatic insertion detection. Requires only tiny sample volume.

On-Board Memory

Allows users to review previous test results directly on the device.

Designed for Every Setting

The HemoScan system utilizes disposable microcuvettes that automatically draw blood by capillary action, making the testing process hygienic, safe, and efficient in any environment.
